
Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 24, 2026

IPTV - Arty ("we", "our", or "the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains what data may be collected when you use the App, how it is used, and what choices you have regarding your data.
1. Information We Collect
User-Provided Playlist Data

The App allows users to add their own M3U playlist URLs, Xtream Codes credentials, or direct stream links in order to access media streams provided by third-party sources.

Playlist credentials are primarily stored locally on your device.

We do not intentionally transmit playlist credentials to our servers unless required for optional cloud synchronization or when you explicitly submit diagnostic information.
Analytics Data

We use Google Analytics for Firebase to collect anonymous usage information such as app launches, feature usage, navigation patterns, and session stability.

This information does not directly identify individual users and is used only to improve app performance and reliability.
Error Reports

Users may optionally send manual support or error reports.

These reports may include:

device model

operating system version

technical logs

user-provided issue descriptions

Sensitive playlist credentials are removed or masked before transmission whenever possible.
Advertising Data

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

AdMob may collect identifiers such as:

Android Advertising ID

Apple Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA)

These identifiers may be used for fraud prevention, ad delivery, and ad personalization depending on your device settings and consent preferences.
Cloud Synchronization

If cloud features are enabled, selected app data such as favorites, settings, and playlist metadata may be securely stored using Supabase infrastructure.
2. How We Use Information

Collected information is used only to:

provide and maintain App functionality

improve performance and stability

analyze crashes and technical issues

support optional cloud synchronization

display advertisements where applicable

We do not sell personal information.
3. Data Storage and Retention

Most user data remains stored locally on your device.

If cloud synchronization is used:

synchronized data is retained only as long as needed to provide the service

users may remove stored data by clearing app data or contacting support

We do not monitor, inspect, or control the content of user-provided playlists or media servers.
4. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

request access to your personal data

request correction or deletion of stored data

withdraw consent for analytics or advertising identifiers
5. Third-Party Services

The App may rely on:

Google Play Services

AdMob

Firebase Analytics / Crashlytics

Supabase

Each provider maintains its own privacy policy.
